GLOBAL MED ENG SOLUTION

GLOBAL MED ENG SOLUTION was established in 2019 and has been serving as a one step solution for all skill and manpower centric needs and is successfully continuing in a bigger way in its current year operations being 5th year of its impact creation. Currently, the team is operative pan Karnataka and is visioned to spread across pan-India presence too in the near future. GMES is promoting skill development centric career development. The organization is aiming to be a leading facilitator in activities that provides a transformative education to create rich experienced individuals delivering expertise services in their fields, enabling education and experience exist together. Currently entering the apprenticeship ecosystem and emphasizing on apprenticeship opportunities by assisting organizations in the industrial / MSME sector and related industries to foster skill development and growth. Thereby working for workforce development and economic growth of this country.

Our Vision

“To be a leading facilitator in activities that provides a transformative education to create rich experienced individuals delivering expertise services in their fields, enabling education and experience exist together. ”

Our Mission

"To be a nurturing ground for an individual's holistic development to make effective contribution to the society."
"To promote demand driven skill development training programs to right hands at the right time and facilitate with right opportunities"
"To promote experiential learning among students, freshers through projects, internship, on the job training, apprenticeship and other policy driven - time driven programs."

Primary Objectives

" To increase the youth’s employability by giving them training and skills tailored to their sector and ensure gainful employment."
" To skill workforce that can contribute to the general growth of the country’s economy."
" To encourage youth in self-employment, nano / micro entrepreneurship through the knowledge and demand driven training."
" Must work with different industries to comprehend their demands and create training programs that meet them, ensuring they are industry relevant and pertinent."
" To assist young people in finding employment after completing training programs by linking them with employers and opening up work prospects including apprenticeship opportunities."

Our Milestones

" GMES has successfully catered job offers to more than 5000+ job aspirants across across Karnataka through the job fair."
" GMES has mobilized more than 8,000+ candidates for various skill and skill centric training programs across Karnataka."
" GMES has been promoting Apprenticeship Fairs in consultation with District Skill Development Offices."
" GMES has collaborated with Industrial Associations such as, Bidadi Industries Association, Peenya Industries Association, Bellary Chamber of Commerce, Jigani Industries Association, Karnataka Small Scale Industries Association for skilled work force."
" GMES has been promoting World Youth Skills Day."
" GMES has been promoting World Skills Competition."
" GMES provides skill development programs in sectors such as IT-ITes, BFSI, Retail, Tourism & Hospitality and pioneering with excellent outcomes."
" GMES has been conducted state wide Career Guidance Programs in collaboration with various education institutions."
" GMES has proposed Special Projects to government bodies in areas of sustainable development goals."
